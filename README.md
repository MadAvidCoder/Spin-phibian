A game where you play as a frog who must break free from the reigns of the Land of _**serious**ness_, and traverse through a treacherous volcano through spinny (and very serious) means, to reach the _Land of Joy and Whimsy_.

See how fast you can complete the game and join the leaderboard!
Platforms: PC, mouse *strongly* recommended.

---
Made for the JuniperDev Very Serious Game Jam

&nbsp;

This is our first (properly) finished game, we hope you enjoy!
